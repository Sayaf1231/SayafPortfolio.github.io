About me

Great To Meet You!!

Hi, my name is Sayaf Kashem and I am a recent grad from SUNY University at Buffalo with a BS in Computer Science. I specialize in taking project ideas and turning them into clean, functional codebases and sometimes even with working tests — wild, I know. My development philosophy emphasizes runtime analysis, object-oriented programming, Dynamic Programming, modular Design, and database management, all grounded in clean, well-documented code. I thrive at the intersection of creativity and technical precision, where ideas become real-world applications that are clean, scalable, and user-focused.

I aspire to be a leader and never back down from responsibilities. I take initiative and responsibility for my work—from idea to deployment. I’ve used and thrived in Agile environments, consistently contributing in team-based settings and never backing down from opportunities to take the lead when needed.
